Crossbow

SILENT · MID-TO-LONG RANGE · HUNTERS & SPECIALISTS

🏹
Overview

The crossbow occupies a unique space in Zehndar's arsenal — precise and powerful like a rifle, but utterly silent. In a world where noise means exposure and exposure means death, the crossbow is the weapon of those who move unseen. It requires no ammo that cannot be retrieved, and leaves no shell casings behind.

Who Carries It

Hunters, scouts, assassins, and wilderness survivors favour the crossbow for its silence and self-sufficiency. It is rarely the weapon of soldiers, but invaluable to those who operate alone or far from supply lines.
