// SIDEARM · CLASS-II

Gun

STANDARD ISSUE · CLOSE-TO-MID RANGE · ALL FACTIONS

Overview

The gun is the most common personal weapon carried across Zehndar's factions. Designed for close to mid-range engagements, it offers speed and reliability over raw stopping power. In the post-apocalyptic landscape of Zehndar, where close-quarters confrontation is inevitable, the pistol has become as much a part of daily life as food and shelter.

Who Carries It

Soldiers, mercenaries, city guards, and Rebels alike carry sidearms across Zehndar. The gun's compact frame makes it the weapon of choice when space and speed matter most.

Known Variants
  • Standard Issue PistolMilitary
  • Silenced FrameCovert
  • Extended Mag VariantModified
  • Hardened Officer's ModelElite
Lore

The gun's of Zehndar are powered by a Dynamo mechanism in the grip. This created power which is stored in energy cells ready to be used to fire the weapon.

Rifle

PRECISION FIRE · LONG RANGE · SOLDIERS & SNIPERS

Overview

The rifle is the weapon of distance, discipline, and patience. Effective far beyond the range of a pistol or blade, it demands skill and composure from those who carry it — and delivers devastating results in the right hands.

Details to be expanded.
